Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 7, 2026 · Last updated: June 7, 2026

The short version. BavarianTranslator has no account, no ads, and no tracking. We run no servers and never receive your data. Your audio is sent only to the translation provider you choose (Google, Groq, or Mistral), using your own API key, for the sole purpose of translating it. Your keys and settings stay on your device.

1. Who we are

BavarianTranslator (“the app”, “we”) is a real-time conversation translator for iOS. This policy explains what data the app handles and how. By using the app you agree to this policy.

2. Information we collect

We, the developer, do not collect, store, or have access to any of your personal information. The app has no user accounts, no login, no analytics or advertising SDKs, and no developer-operated backend server. We cannot see your conversations, your audio, or your translations.

3. Microphone & audio

The app uses your device microphone only while you are actively using it to translate — when you tap to speak (Tap mode) or while a hands-free mode (Auto/Live) is switched on. The captured audio is sent directly from your device to the cloud translation provider you selected, using your own API key, for the single purpose of transcribing and translating it. The app does not record your audio to a file, does not keep it after the translation is returned, and never sends it to us.

4. Third-party translation providers

To translate, the app sends your audio to whichever provider you enable in Settings. Your use of that provider is governed by their terms and privacy policy, not ours:

ProviderWhat is sentTheir privacy policy
Google GeminiYour audio clipai.google.dev/gemini-api/terms
GroqYour audio clipgroq.com/privacy-policy
MistralYour audio clipmistral.ai/terms
ElevenLabs (optional)German text, only if you enable itelevenlabs.io/privacy

You choose the provider, and you supply your own API key, so the request is made under your own account with that provider. We recommend reviewing the privacy policy of whichever provider you use. The optional ElevenLabs voice feature is off by default; if you turn it on, only the German text to be spoken is sent to ElevenLabs.

5. Data stored on your device

The following is stored locally on your device only, and is never transmitted to us:

  • API keys you enter, and your app settings (chosen engine, voice, speed, mode), saved on the device so you don’t have to re-enter them.
  • Conversation text shown on screen is held only in the app’s memory during a session and is cleared when you tap the trash button or close the app. It is not written to a file.

You can remove all of this at any time by clearing the conversation in-app and/or deleting the app from your device.

6. How your information is used

Audio you speak is used solely to produce a translation, in real time, via the provider you selected. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, model training, or any secondary purpose, and we do not sell or share it — because we never receive it.

7. Children’s privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from them. Because the app collects no personal information at all, no such data is gathered from any user.

8. International users

The translation provider you choose may process your audio on servers located in other countries. Please review that provider’s privacy policy (linked above) for details on international data handling.

9. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and revise the “Last updated” date above. Material changes will be reflected here before they take effect.
